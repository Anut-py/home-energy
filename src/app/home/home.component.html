Hello!

This website is for a seventh grade science project.

In this website, you will learn about all the parts of an energy efficient home.

Each part will have an interactive 3d model next to it.

It will also have some images, because the interactive 3d models look worse than the original 3d models. The images will show what the original models looked like.

To see each part and how it affects energy transfer, click on the View button at the top of the screen.

To view all of the 3d models and images without any text, click on the Gallery button.
